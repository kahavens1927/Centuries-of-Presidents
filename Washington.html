Important Dates

  • Born February 22, 1732 in Westmoreland County, VA
  • In June 1775, Washington was appointed Commander-in-Chief of the Continental Army
  • On April 16, 1789, he was inagurated as the first president of the United States
  • Died December 14, 1799

Bio

George Washington was born to slave owners in Virginia and when his father died, he became a slave owner and the head of his household at age 11. Because of this, he was without a formal education. His military career started early and was appointed to the position of Major at age 21. He married in 1758 and spent the next few decades growing his wealth. In 1787, he started down the path to become president by being elected as a delegate to the Constitutional Convention.
