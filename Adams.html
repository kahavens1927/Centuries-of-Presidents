Important Dates

  • Born October 30, 1735 in Braintree, Massachusets
  • October 1770, Adams gained fame for defending the British soldiers
    who were charged for the Boston Massacre
  • Adams son, John Quincy Adams, became president in 1824.
  • Died July 4, 1826

Bio

Adams spent his early life with a good education and eventually went to Harvard. Early in his political
career, Adams emerged as the leader of the pro-independence faction in Congress and nominated
George Washington of Virginia to Commander-in-Cheif. Adams returned home in 1779
and authored the Massachusetts state constitution, the longest running state
constitution in the nation. John Adams and Thomas Jefferson both died on July 4, 1826.
